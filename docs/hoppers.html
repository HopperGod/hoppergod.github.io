Hopper Cone Information


Efficient grain handling is crucial for maximizing productivity. Custom built cones can play a vital role in streamlining grain transfer and storage. Wether it be a special aeration, widened legs for a larger auger, or a steep slope up to 40 degrees we have the options for you.
Each cone is also designed to have equal amount of legs to stiffners if stiffners were part of the order. Here's an information table of some of the things we can do.

Warning: The skid base may undergo vertical movement due to unloading and loading cycles of the bin. Differential movement of one hopper leg to another causes stress in the hopper system. If the differential movement exceeds 0.4 inches between any two legs of the hopper, then unload the bin and repair the foundation to ensure differential leg settlement cannot occur further.

Note: Base pressure will change based on contents.
