U-Dumps

The U-DUMP is engineered to have minimal hitch weight and can be towed behind most vehicles. They are exceptionally well-balanced, safe, and easy to use. With a turn of the winch handle you can dump the full 2,800-pound capacity in seconds. The U-DUMP comes with industrial grade plastic lids, removable anti-theft hitch, and a simple winch-controlled dumping system.

What can a U-DUMP be used for?

  • Construction Site Cleanup
  • Roofing Jobs (shingles)
  • Weddings or Yard Parties
  • Ball Tournaments
  • Home Renovations
  • Livestock Feed

The U-DUMP is also Excellent for:

  • Rural Municipality
  • Provincial Parks
  • Campgrounds
  • Trailer Courts
  • Acreages

Dump all your troubles away with the versitile and dependable U-DUMP!
