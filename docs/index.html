About our Company

We have proudly served our customers for over 30 years, delivering high-quality products and exceptional service. With a network of over 50 dealers across Alberta, Saskatchewan, and Manitoba, we are a trusted name in the industry. Thanks to our skilled and experienced team, we are capable of producing over 1,500 hopper cones annually. All our hopper cones are designed by the team at Method Engineering & Design Inc.

Capabilities and Machines

Our 36,000-square-foot welding facility is just one of several buildings on our recently expanded 15-acre worksite. Equipped with high-quality machinery, we ensure every job is completed with precision and efficiency. Below are our machines in operation:

Yaskawa AR3120 Welding Robot

Our state-of-the-art Yaskawa robot delivers precise, high-speed performance. It is best suited for repetitive parts, such as the skid sections on our hopper cones. With an impressive 12-foot arm length, it can easily handle even the largest skid sections, including those on our 33-foot hopper cones.

Hypertherm CNC Plasma Table

This 400 Amp high-definition plasma table is capable of cutting material up to 3" thick. The rotating torch head allows us to cut bevels on plate up to 45°. With a workable cutting area of 8x12 feet, we can handle a wide variety of projects.

Omada VT5063

Horizontal Band Saw capable of cutting up to 20 inch diameter metal tubing with a 60 degree swing in either direction.

Geka Ironworker

Multipurpose machine used for punching holes and shearing angle iron, flat bar, as well as round bar.

Fabmaster Horizontal Bender

Great for bending small parts and tight boxes. Capacity ranges from 14 gauge to 1/2 plate.

DAVI 4-Roll

Rolls plate, cylinders, and cones up to 6 feet wide. Thickness ranges from 14 gauge up to 1 inch depending on width.
